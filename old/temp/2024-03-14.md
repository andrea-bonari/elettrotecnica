### metodo delle prove semplici

- equivalente di Thevenin
$$V=R_{TH}i+E_{TH}$$
![[Pasted image 20240314111740.png]]

$$T\bigg|_{i=0}=E_{TH}$$
- equivalente di Norton
$$i=G_{NR}V+A_{NR}$$
![[Pasted image 20240314112236.png]]
$$\frac{i}{V}\bigg|_{A_{NR}=0}=G_{NR}$$
$$i\bigg|_{V=0}A_{RN}$$
